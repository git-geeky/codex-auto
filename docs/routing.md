# Routing

Precedence is explicit CLI lane, task-spec lane, repository rule, high-risk match, mechanical positive match, then standard. Mechanical never follows from prompt length alone. Latency and bounded-hard are explicit by default. A failed Luna route never falls to Terra.

Transient failures retry the same tier with a separate bounded budget. Credentials, permissions, sandbox, environment, configuration, and contradictory-spec blockers stop. One same-tier substantive repair requires new external evidence, a nonrepeated fingerprint, measurable progress, and remaining budget.

Luna Medium mechanical work may move to Luna High only with localized evidence; otherwise it goes to Sol High. Luna High and Terra Medium rescue to Sol High. Sol High selects exactly one final Max or Ultra route. Model self-report never selects Ultra.
